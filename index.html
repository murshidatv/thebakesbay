    Our Story

    from home baker to entrepreanur

    Behind every cake...,there is a story - a story of hardwork, long hours of baking, frosting, decorating, late night or sleepless nights...,inevitable backpain/neckpain/even hand aches...the stress that can come out of nowhere...

    But all that pain,tiredness and stress goes away in a jiffy with a positive feedback from our Happy customers.. It is the best part of baking a cake or desert..

    Happiness is being able to bring thosesmiles and gestures of satisfaction just through our cakes.. And the best part we enjoy is the surprise cakes and gifts that are commissioned to surprise the customer's family or friends.
